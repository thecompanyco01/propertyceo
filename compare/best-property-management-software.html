7 Best Property Management Software in 2026 (Honest Rankings)

1. AppFolio — Best for Growing PM Companies

AppFolio is the most feature-rich PM software on the market. Its AI-powered tools for maintenance triage, leasing automation, and owner reporting set it apart from the competition. The 50-unit minimum and per-unit pricing mean it's not for everyone, but if you're managing 200+ doors and growing, AppFolio gives you the tools to scale efficiently.

Why we rank it #1: The AI features aren't gimmicks — they genuinely save hours per week on maintenance routing, listing creation, and tenant screening. For a growing PM company, that time savings translates directly into more doors managed per employee.

  • Best feature: AI maintenance triage that auto-categorizes and routes work orders
  • Biggest weakness: 50-unit minimum excludes smaller PMs
  • Price: $1.40/unit/mo (Core), $3/unit/mo (Plus)

2. Buildium — Best for Small to Mid-Size PMs

Buildium is the workhorse of PM software. It does everything you need without overcomplicating things. No unit minimums, affordable pricing, and a clean interface make it the go-to choice for PM companies under 500 doors. Setup takes a day, not weeks.

Why we rank it #2: For the price, Buildium delivers incredible value. The accounting module is solid, tenant screening is built in, and the learning curve is gentle. It's the safe, reliable choice.

  • Best feature: Simple setup — you can be operational in a day
  • Biggest weakness: Dated interface, no AI features yet
  • Price: $58/mo (Essential), $183/mo (Growth), $375/mo (Premium)

3. Rent Manager — Best for Large Portfolios

Rent Manager is the power tool of PM software. It's highly customizable and can handle the complexity of large, mixed portfolios. The tradeoff is a steep learning curve and longer implementation timeline. But for PMs managing 500+ doors across residential, commercial, and mixed-use — Rent Manager's flexibility is unmatched.

  • Best feature: Unlimited customization of workflows, reports, and fields
  • Biggest weakness: Complex setup, steeper learning curve
  • Price: Custom (contact for quote, typically $1-2/unit/mo)

4. Propertyware — Best for Single-Family Portfolios

If your portfolio is primarily single-family homes, Propertyware is built specifically for you. It excels at managing scattered-site portfolios with features like drive time optimization for inspections and maintenance. Owned by RealPage, it also integrates well with their broader ecosystem.

  • Best feature: Single-family-specific tools (drive time routing, inspection scheduling)
  • Biggest weakness: Not great for multi-family or mixed portfolios
  • Price: $1/unit/mo (Basic), $1.50/unit/mo (Plus), $2/unit/mo (Premium)

5. TenantCloud — Best Free/Budget Option

TenantCloud offers a genuinely useful free plan for landlords managing up to 75 units. The paid plans are also very affordable. It lacks the depth of Buildium or AppFolio, but for solo operators or very small PM companies just starting out, it's a solid stepping stone.

  • Best feature: Free plan that actually works for small portfolios
  • Biggest weakness: Limited reporting and automation at scale
  • Price: Free (up to 75 units), $15/mo (Starter), $35/mo (Growth)

6. Yardi Breeze — Best for Mixed Portfolios

Yardi Breeze is the lightweight version of Yardi Voyager (the enterprise giant). It handles residential, commercial, affordable housing, and mixed-use portfolios well. The interface is modern and the pricing is competitive, though it's not as intuitive as Buildium for beginners.

  • Best feature: Handles every property type (residential, commercial, affordable housing)
  • Biggest weakness: Can feel like a stripped-down enterprise tool
  • Price: $1/unit/mo (Breeze), $2/unit/mo (Breeze Premier)

7. Hemlane — Best for Hybrid Management

Hemlane is unique — it offers a hybrid model where you can manage some tasks yourself and outsource others (like maintenance coordination) to Hemlane's team. This is great for landlords transitioning to PM or PMs who want to offer tiered service levels.

  • Best feature: Built-in maintenance coordination service
  • Biggest weakness: Less suitable for fully self-managed portfolios
  • Price: $2/unit/mo (Basic), $32/unit/mo (Complete with maintenance coordination)

How to Choose: Our Decision Framework

Stop overthinking this. Here's the simple framework:

  • Under 50 doors: Buildium Essential or TenantCloud
  • 50-200 doors: Buildium Growth or AppFolio Core
  • 200-500 doors: AppFolio Core or Plus
  • 500+ doors: AppFolio Plus, Rent Manager, or Yardi Breeze
  • Single-family specialist: Propertyware
  • Budget-conscious: TenantCloud or Buildium Essential

The Truth About PM Software

Here's what most comparison articles won't tell you: your PM software is maybe 10% of what determines your success. We've seen PM companies crushing it on spreadsheets and others failing with the best software in the world.

What actually determines your growth is your owner acquisition system, your pricing strategy, your team, and your operational efficiency. The software is just a tool — and any of the top 3 on this list will serve you well.

Pick one. Set it up. Then spend your time on what actually matters: getting more doors and keeping owners happy.
