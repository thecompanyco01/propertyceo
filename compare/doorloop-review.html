What Is DoorLoop?

DoorLoop is a cloud-based property management platform that covers the full stack: tenant management, lease tracking, rent collection, accounting, maintenance, owner reporting, and marketing. It handles residential, commercial, student housing, HOA, and mixed-use portfolios — a versatility that many competitors lack at this price point.

The company was founded by property managers who were frustrated with the existing options. That origin story shows — the UI is noticeably more modern than Buildium or Rent Manager, and the onboarding is smoother than AppFolio's multi-week process.

DoorLoop Features: What Actually Works

User Interface & Design (⭐ Excellent)

This is where DoorLoop genuinely shines. The interface is clean, modern, and intuitive. If you've ever used Buildium or Rent Manager and felt like you stepped back to 2010, DoorLoop will feel like a breath of fresh air. Dashboards are well-designed, navigation is logical, and things are where you'd expect them to be.

Setup took us about 2 hours to import our test portfolio of 50 units. They offer free data migration assistance, which is a nice touch.

Accounting (⭐ Very Good)

DoorLoop's accounting is surprisingly robust for a newer platform. It supports trust/escrow accounting, bank reconciliation, 1099 e-filing, CAM charges, and multi-entity reporting. The chart of accounts is customizable, and reports are clean and exportable.

It's not as deep as Rent Manager's accounting (which is basically a mini-ERP), but it's on par with Buildium and significantly better than free tools like TurboTenant or Stessa.

Rent Collection (⭐ Good)

Standard ACH and credit card payment processing, autopay setup, late fee automation, and partial payment tracking. Nothing groundbreaking, but reliable. Payments are processed through Stripe, which is proven and secure.

ACH processing takes 2-3 business days. Credit card fees (2.99%) are passed to tenants. Compared to AppFolio, which offers same-day ACH on higher tiers, DoorLoop's payment processing is standard but not leading.

Owner Portal (⭐ Good — Pro plan only)

The owner portal is clean and gives owners access to financial statements, maintenance updates, and documents. It's not as feature-rich as AppFolio's owner portal (which offers custom branding and detailed investment analysis), but it covers the essentials that third-party PM companies need.

Maintenance Management (⭐ Good)

Tenants submit requests through the portal, PMs track status, assign vendors, and attach photos/documents. The Pro plan adds automated workflows and vendor management. It works well for standard maintenance operations.

Missing: AI triage (AppFolio has this), automated dispatching to preferred vendors, and preventative maintenance scheduling. These are nice-to-haves, not dealbreakers.

Reporting & Analytics (⭐ Decent)

DoorLoop offers standard PM reports — rent roll, vacancy, income/expense, owner statements, aging receivables. The Premium plan adds custom report building. Reports are clean and exportable to Excel/PDF.

Compared to AppFolio's analytics suite or Buildium's performance insights, DoorLoop's reporting is functional but not a differentiator.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does DoorLoop offer a free trial?

DoorLoop offers a free demo but no self-serve free trial. You'll need to schedule a call with their sales team to get started. They do offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans.

Can DoorLoop handle HOA management?

Yes. DoorLoop supports HOA/condo management with dues tracking, violation management, and board portals. This is a differentiator — many competitors at this price point don't offer HOA features.

How does DoorLoop's customer support compare?

DoorLoop offers live chat, email, and phone support. Response times are generally fast (under 1 hour for chat). They also have an extensive knowledge base and video tutorials. Support quality is consistently rated highly in user reviews.

Is DoorLoop good for single-family rentals?

Yes, but it's overkill if you only have 1-5 single-family homes. At $59/mo minimum, you're paying $12-60 per door — far more expensive per unit than free options like TurboTenant. DoorLoop makes more financial sense starting at 15-20+ units.

Can I migrate from Buildium to DoorLoop?

Yes. DoorLoop offers free data migration assistance. They'll help transfer your tenant data, lease information, and financial records. Budget 1-2 weeks for the full transition. The migration process is smoother than moving to/from AppFolio because DoorLoop's team is actively involved.
