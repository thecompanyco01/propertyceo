Hemlane Review 2026: The Hybrid Software + Services Platform for Remote Landlords

4.0/5
PropertyCEO Rating

Best for: Remote landlords and small portfolio owners who want software AND hands-on management help

Price: Free (Starter) | Basic $30/mo | Essential $48/mo | Complete $86/mo

Hemlane takes a fundamentally different approach to property management software. While platforms like TurboTenant and Avail give you tools and say "manage it yourself," Hemlane combines software with actual human services — repair coordination, tenant communication, and even local leasing help.

This hybrid model makes Hemlane uniquely valuable for one specific group: landlords who live far from their rental properties and want more than software but less than a full property manager. If that's you, keep reading. If you're a hands-on local landlord, you might be better served by simpler (and cheaper) tools.

🏢 Company: Founded in 2015 in San Francisco, CA. Backed by Y Combinator (W16 batch). Serves landlords across all 50 US states.

💰 Pricing: Free Starter | Basic $30/mo ($2/unit + $28 base) | Essential $48/mo ($20/unit + $28 base) | Complete $86/mo ($58/unit + $28 base)

🎯 Target User: Remote/out-of-state landlords with 1-100 units who want built-in management assistance

⚡ Best Feature: 24/7 repair coordination with emergency support — real humans handling your maintenance calls

What Is Hemlane?

Hemlane is a property management platform that bridges the gap between DIY landlord software and full-service property management. At its core, it's software — listing, screening, leasing, rent collection, accounting. But on its higher-tier plans, it layers in human services: a dedicated repair coordinator handles maintenance requests, an assistant manages tenant communications, and local agents can handle leasing and turnovers.

This is different from anything else in the market. TurboTenant, Avail, and Innago give you tools. Buildium and AppFolio give you enterprise tools. Hemlane gives you tools plus people who help you use them. Think of it as property management with training wheels — you stay in control, but you're not doing everything alone.

Hemlane is a Y Combinator graduate (Winter 2016 batch), which signals strong product thinking and ambition. The team is based in San Francisco and has been steadily growing the platform and services network since launch.

Hemlane Features: What Actually Works

Repair Coordination (⭐ Excellent — The Killer Feature)

This is why people choose Hemlane. On the Essential and Complete plans, you get a dedicated repair coordinator who handles maintenance requests 24/7, including emergencies. When a tenant submits a request, Hemlane's team:

  1. Triages the request and diagnoses the issue
  2. Troubleshoots with the tenant to see if it's a simple fix
  3. If a professional is needed, coordinates with your preferred vendors (or their vetted network)
  4. Manages the work order through completion
  5. Handles invoicing and payment — with no markups on repairs

For remote landlords, this is transformative. No more 2 AM phone calls about a leaking toilet. No more trying to coordinate repairs from 3,000 miles away. Hemlane handles it, and you just approve the cost. The "no markup" policy is important — some competing services add 10-20% to repair costs.

Accounting & Financial Tracking (⭐ Very Good)

Hemlane offers solid accounting features even on the free Starter plan — bank account syncing, custom categorization, income/expense tracking, cash flow reports, income statements, and lease ledgers. This is notably better than what TurboTenant or Avail offer at any price point.

You can connect your bank accounts, credit cards, and other financial accounts, then categorize transactions and generate tax-ready reports. It's not as deep as Buildium's full GL with trust accounting, but for independent landlords it's more than sufficient.

Tenant Screening (⭐ Very Good)

Hemlane uses a proprietary screening questionnaire combined with TransUnion credit reports, nationwide criminal and eviction screening, and income verification. The "Tenant Risk Analysis" feature provides an overall risk assessment that's helpful for less experienced landlords who aren't sure how to interpret raw credit data.

Listing & Marketing (⭐ Good)

Listings syndicate to Apartments.com, Zillow, and 7+ other major sites. Each listing gets its own page where tenants can schedule showings — a nice touch for remote landlords who can't be there to show units. Listing details are saved for future turnovers, reducing the work needed when a tenant moves out.

Rent Collection (⭐ Good — Basic plan and above)

ACH payments are free (no transaction fees) on Basic, Essential, and Complete plans. Tenants can also pay by debit or credit card (3% fee). Automatic payment reminders and late fee enforcement are included. Peer-to-peer payments (like Venmo or Zelle) can be tracked within the platform.

Tenant Placement Service (⭐ Good Add-On)

For $695+, Hemlane's team will find and place a tenant for you — marketing the unit, qualifying applicants, scheduling tours, and completing the placement. This is ideal for remote landlords who can't physically show a property. The price is competitive with local leasing agents who typically charge one month's rent.

Who Should Use Hemlane

  • Budget-minimum landlords — if you want 100% free, use TurboTenant or Innago
  • Commercial property owners — residential only

Hemlane vs TurboTenant: Completely different philosophies. TurboTenant is free DIY tools; Hemlane is software + services. If you're local and hands-on, TurboTenant is the better value. If you're remote or hate maintenance calls, Hemlane's repair coordination is worth every penny.

Our Verdict: 4.0/5

Hemlane is the best option for landlords who want something between DIY software and full property management. The hybrid model is genuinely innovative — repair coordination, tenant placement, and management assistance at a fraction of what a traditional PM company charges.

The main limitations are scale (gets expensive past 20 units) and scope (residential only, no owner portal). But for its target audience — remote landlords with 1-20 units who want help without giving up control — Hemlane is uniquely excellent.

Bottom line: If you live far from your rental properties and dread that midnight maintenance call, Hemlane is the answer. If you're local and hands-on, save your money and use TurboTenant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hemlane a property management company?

Not exactly. Hemlane is a software platform that includes property management services. You stay in control of your properties — Hemlane provides tools and human assistance. They don't take over management of your property the way a traditional PM company would. Think of it as "management-assisted" rather than "fully managed."

Is Hemlane's free plan worth using?

The Starter (free) plan includes listing syndication, tenant screening, and basic accounting — but no rent collection or lease management. It's good for getting started, but most landlords will want at least the Basic plan ($30/mo) for rent collection and lease features. The free plan is best used as a trial to see if you like the platform.

How does Hemlane's repair coordination work?

On Essential and Complete plans, tenants submit maintenance requests through the platform. Hemlane's repair team (real people, available 24/7) triages the request, troubleshoots with the tenant, and coordinates with service professionals if needed. You set price thresholds — repairs under your limit are handled automatically; above it, they get your approval first. No markup on repair costs.

Is Hemlane cheaper than a property manager?

Yes, significantly. A traditional PM company charges 8-12% of monthly rent (plus leasing fees, maintenance markups, etc.). Hemlane's Complete plan at $58/unit/month provides similar services at roughly half the cost. The tradeoff is that you're still involved in decision-making — you don't fully hand off management.

Can Hemlane handle multi-family properties?

Yes. Hemlane supports single-family, multi-family, and apartment rentals. Each unit is priced separately, so a 4-unit building on the Essential plan would be $28 base + ($20 × 4) = $108/month. The repair coordination and other services apply per-unit.

Does Hemlane work nationwide?

Yes. Hemlane serves landlords in all 50 US states. Their repair coordination network covers most metro areas, though vendor availability may be more limited in rural areas. State-specific leases are available on Essential and Complete plans.
