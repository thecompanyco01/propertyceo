What Is Rent Manager?

Rent Manager is an enterprise property management platform built by London Computer Systems (LCS), a company that's been in the PM software business since 1987. That's nearly 40 years of development — and it shows in the depth of the platform.

While most PM software companies try to simplify everything (sometimes at the cost of power), Rent Manager takes the opposite approach. It gives you granular control over nearly every aspect of property management — from accounting entries to maintenance workflows to reporting formats. You can configure Rent Manager to match exactly how your business operates, rather than conforming your business to how the software works.

This approach has a tradeoff: Rent Manager has a steeper learning curve than Buildium or AppFolio. But for companies that need that depth, no other platform comes close.

Rent Manager Features: What Actually Works

Accounting & Financial Reporting (⭐ Exceptional)

This is where Rent Manager dominates. The platform includes a complete, enterprise-grade accounting system with trust accounting, bank reconciliation, AP/AR management, and — here's the kicker — 450+ built-in financial and property-related reports.

That's not a typo. Four hundred and fifty reports. From standard P&L and balance sheets to specialized property performance metrics, delinquency analysis, vacancy loss tracking, and owner disbursement summaries. If you need a report, Rent Manager almost certainly has it built in. And if it doesn't, you can create custom reports.

For PM companies with demanding owners or institutional investors who need specific reporting formats, this depth is invaluable. It's the primary reason companies choose Rent Manager over AppFolio or Buildium.

Customization (⭐ Exceptional)

Rent Manager offers unlimited custom fields on virtually every data object — properties, units, tenants, owners, vendors, work orders. This means you can track any piece of data your business needs without workarounds or spreadsheets.

Combined with customizable user roles and permissions, workflow automations, and the available API, Rent Manager can be configured to match almost any business process. Some PM companies build entire custom workflows that are unique to their operation — something that's simply not possible on more rigid platforms.

Maintenance & Work Order Management (⭐ Excellent)

Rent Manager's maintenance system is comprehensive: work order creation and tracking, vendor management with no-cost vendor pay, PO system with inventory management, service tech mapping and scheduling, and video inspections. The rmAppSuite Pro mobile app lets field techs manage work orders, take photos, get directions, and update status from the field.

The service tech mapping feature is particularly useful — it shows you where your maintenance staff is relative to open work orders, helping you route technicians efficiently. For companies with in-house maintenance teams, this is a genuine time-saver.

Marketing & Leasing (⭐ Very Good)

Rent Manager includes comprehensive marketing and leasing tools — listing syndication, online applications, prospect management, unlimited eSignatures, and prospect/owner/tenant portals. The leasing workflow handles the full cycle from marketing a vacancy through move-in.

The survey analytics hub is a nice addition — it lets you gather feedback from tenants, prospects, and owners, which helps improve retention and identify issues before they become problems.

Orion AI (⭐ Promising — Premium Bundle)

Rent Manager's AI features, branded as "Orion AI," include a writing assistant for drafting communications and a Smart AR/AP suite that automates accounts receivable and payable workflows. These are Premium bundle features and are still maturing, but they show that LCS is investing in AI capabilities. The writing assistant is useful for quickly drafting professional notices, emails, and communications.

Mobile App — rmAppSuite Pro (⭐ Very Good)

The rmAppSuite Pro mobile app is more robust than most PM software mobile apps. It's designed for field work — inspections, maintenance management, and property walkthroughs. The app includes mapping and routing features that are particularly useful for maintenance teams covering a geographic area.

Communications (⭐ Good)

Rent Manager includes text and phone broadcast capabilities (with a text-enabled phone number subscription). You can send bulk notifications to tenants, owners, or vendors — useful for weather emergencies, policy changes, or community announcements. Combined with the portals, it covers most communication needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Rent Manager cost?

Rent Manager doesn't publish pricing. You need to request a custom quote based on your portfolio size and feature needs. Pricing is typically per-unit-per-month with a separate implementation fee (2× your monthly fee). Contact Rent Manager directly for a quote — and don't be afraid to negotiate, especially for larger portfolios.

Does Rent Manager have a free trial?

No. Rent Manager offers comprehensive demos and guided walkthroughs, but no free trial. Given the complexity of the platform and the implementation process, a trial wouldn't be very useful anyway — you need proper setup and training to evaluate it fairly.

Is Rent Manager better than AppFolio?

They're different tools for different priorities. Rent Manager is more customizable with deeper reporting (450+ reports vs. AppFolio's standard set). AppFolio has a more modern interface and more advanced AI features. Choose Rent Manager if customization and reporting are your top priorities. Choose AppFolio if user experience and AI capabilities matter more.

Can Rent Manager handle commercial properties?

Yes — and it handles them well. Rent Manager supports residential, commercial, mixed-use, student housing, affordable housing, and HOA portfolios. The unlimited custom fields mean you can track commercial-specific data (CAM charges, lease escalations, tenant improvements) without workarounds.

How long does Rent Manager implementation take?

Expect 6-12 weeks for full implementation, including data migration, system configuration, and staff training. Larger portfolios or complex migrations take longer. The implementation fee (2× your monthly fee) covers a dedicated team to help you get set up. Plan for significant time investment from your team during this period.

Is Rent Manager cloud-based?

Yes. Rent Manager is a cloud-based (SaaS) platform accessible via web browser. They also offer the rmAppSuite Pro mobile app for field operations. No local installation is required.
