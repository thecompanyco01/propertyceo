Buildium Pricing 2026: Plans, Costs & Is It Worth It?

Buildium is one of the most popular property management software options for small to mid-size PM companies. But which plan do you actually need, and is it worth the cost? Here's our complete pricing breakdown.

Buildium Pricing Plans

PlanPriceUnitsBest For
Essential$58/moUp to 150Small PMs just starting out
Growth$183/moUp to 5,000Growing PM companies
Premium$375/moUnlimitedLarger operations wanting API access

What's Included in Each Plan

Essential ($58/mo)

  • Accounting & financial reporting
  • Online rent collection (ACH & credit card)
  • Maintenance request tracking
  • Tenant & lease tracking
  • 1099 e-filing
  • Tenant screening
  • Online rental applications
  • Buildium website

Growth ($183/mo) — Everything in Essential, plus:

  • Performance analytics dashboard
  • eSignatures for leases
  • Priority support
  • Property inspections
  • Buildium Rewards program

Premium ($375/mo) — Everything in Growth, plus:

  • Open API access
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Priority listing on Buildium's renter search

Key insight: Buildium becomes dramatically cheaper per door as your portfolio grows. At 500 doors, you're paying $0.37/door vs AppFolio's $1.40/door. That's a $450/mo savings.

Is Buildium Worth It?

Yes, if you're under 500 doors. Buildium is the best value in PM software for small to mid-size operators. The Essential plan at $58/mo is genuinely hard to beat — it covers all the basics you need to run a professional operation.

When to consider upgrading: Move to Growth ($183/mo) when you pass 150 doors or when you need eSignatures and performance analytics. The Premium plan ($375/mo) is only worth it if you need API access for integrations.

When to consider alternatives: If you're past 500 doors and feeling constrained, look at AppFolio or Rent Manager. Buildium works at 500+, but more advanced platforms might save your team enough time to justify the higher cost.
